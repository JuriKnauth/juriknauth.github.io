Homework 2015-11-18

My three favorite games


Mashed
Mashed is a superb party-game. It is like Micro Machines or other racers of the kind, in which you have a chance to collect weapons and shoot adversaries. The main feature is that all players share a screen and whenever one of them drives too far ahead; he already wins. Likewise, if a player falls too far behind; he is out. This feature guarantees short exiting rounds. The cars also have some vehicle mechanics and skilled drivers can force other players of the road.


Mashed Ingame

Mashed Score

Metro
In Metro: Last Light you immerse into a post-apocalyptic world. The entire game is throughout from the ego-perspective and doesn’t even switch for cut scenes, which are in most cases quick time events. The whole setting is rich and convincing. After a nuclear clash, the remaining survivors hold out in underground stations. The surface is so contaminated, that you can only breath with a gas mask, which glass can get steamy and shatter. The game mode “Ranger” doesn’t have a HUD at all, so the player must count bullets to survive.


Metro quick-time-event

Metro Ingame

Open Liero X
Open Liero X is the ultimate hot-seat game, in which siblings and friends can compete. Liero is a real-time version of Worms, the key difference is that each player only controls one worm. Skilled players learn to give a cut to their projectiles, while they fly around with their ninja ropes. The original download already comes with a bundle of weapon mods and many settings to tweak the game. The settings menu has a slider to show more options and features, ranging from basic to unstable.
