Game Jam: Chick Lights Out

►

Submission to the three day Coding Blocks Jam '23 hosted by Coding Blocks.

There are five rules.

When you flip a rule, one or two other rules are flipped. The rules are also flipped at certain intervals, as well as the way they are wired. The rules can be flipped using the number keys 1 to 5 on the keyboard.
  1. W: Jump || Don't jump
  2. A: Left || Right, D: Right || Left
  3. Shiftt: Sprint || Walk
  4. Egg: Gain life || Lose life
  5. Apple: Gain points || Lose points

Game Jam: Cat and Mouse

►

Submission to Berlin Mini Jam. Created within 10 hours.

This game is split into two phases: In the first phase, the mouse gets to run away and paint a path. In the second phase, the cat has to trace the path. take turns; alternating who paints the path first, and who traces it second. Player one is blue and player two is orange. The player wins who has traced the previous path of the mouse closer.

Be aware of the objects flying around, as they will freeze whoever bumps into it into place! You can play with a game-pad (Left-Stick) or the arrow keys on your keyboard!

This game was created at the Berlin Mini Jam - so within 8 hours. The theme was "I see what you did there", "Long forgotten" and "That's cheating!". So there is an easy, very unsportsmanlike way to cheat, which we left in the game for you to discover!

Storyboard thumbs: Im Westen nichts Neues, Kapitel XI Auszug

►

“All Quiet on the Western Front, excerpt from chapter XI".

First storyboard thumbnails I did. "Im Westen nichts Neues" by Erich Maria Remarque is the most frequently translated German novel. I highly recommend it! It's about a German soldier and his struggle to survive in the trenches of the First World War. It paints a very realistic picture of the cruelties and senseless killings at the front, where people are transformed into animals - only looking to find the next meal and shelter from the cold. Bullets are just another cause of death. These boys are lost in the mud and even those who escape the shrapnel are scared for life.

In this scene the last remaining friend of the main character is wounded.
