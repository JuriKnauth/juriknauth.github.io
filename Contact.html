Motivation

Since a small kid I paid special attention to games creation and started building my own sequels, whenever I thought the original to be disappointing. Today I focus on creating new titles and unique mechanics. The pursue of creating believable worlds and discovering innovative and fun ways of interaction thrill me. Seeing the joy and affection your work can bestow on others is bringing a smile to my face every time. Most of my time I am generating new ideas or guide friends to pinpoint the game that they envision. I consider creating and designing games that are really working well to be my mayor strength, for this I iterate many versions of it in my head to get a leg up for the production. Once production starts, I try to help wherever I can and am eager to learn whatever is necessary to finish the product.
