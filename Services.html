First you get lost in the woods. Then someone comes and tries to find you.

In the first phase you get lost in the forest. Walk around with the WASD keys and use the mouse. Keep screaming for help and try to mesmerize your surroundings. Try to create a footprint trail that is easy to spot and follow. In the second phase, try to find the person from the first phase. His movements and calls for help will be replayed. Try to find him before the time runs out.

My dad is quite the mountain biker. I prototyped a mountain bike simulator with his own 360° footage and his KickR.
The game is fairly straightforward: the replay speed of the video-footage is adjusted to the torque of the KickR. Another feature is to adjust the resistance of the KickR to an elevation-data-track at the same time.
