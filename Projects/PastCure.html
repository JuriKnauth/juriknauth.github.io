Past Cure

A gripping psychological thriller from Phantom 8 Studios. Past Cure puts players in the shoes of former soldier turned vigilante Ian as he attempts to uncover his mysterious past while evading twisted enemies and unraveling sinister conspiracies. With a compelling story and superb visuals, Past Cure offers an experience that is sure to captivate fans of psychological horror games.



The gameplay of Past Cure is a mix of stealth, action and puzzle-solving. The player controls Ian, a former soldier with psychic abilities, as he tries to piece together his memories and find out who is responsible for the experiments that were conducted on him.
In the game, Ian has a number of abilities at his disposal, such as telekinesis and mind control. These abilities can be used to help him navigate the environment and solve puzzles. For example, telekinesis can be used to move objects out of the way or to create distractions, while mind control can be used to take control of enemies and make them do your bidding.
The stealth element comes into play when Ian is trying to avoid detection by enemies. He can use his abilities to stay hidden or to take out enemies silently. However, if he is spotted, then the enemy will sound the alarm and all hell will break loose.
Overall, the gameplay of Past Cure is tense and thrilling, with plenty of challenges to keep players engaged. It's a unique blend of genres that should appeal to fans of both action games and puzzle games.
