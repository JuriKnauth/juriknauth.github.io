Ossel's Odyssey

A quirky 2.5D platformer.

Originally created by Oliver Stephan (creaturelab3d), Ossel’s Odyssey is a side-scrolling platformer in the spirit of classic Mario-style games—but with its own absurd, dry sense of humor. Players can discover odd graffiti, unlock music tracks, and stumble across unexpected surprises that might make you blow air through your nose. Now ported from Unity 4 to Unity 6 with upgraded mechanics and fresh code, the game also includes a modern high score system and new features while preserving its original charm.

Right-to-Left (RTL) Language Support

To ensure a smooth and inclusive experience for all players, I implemented full RTL support for Arabic. This included adapting layouts, mirroring key UI components, and validating language rendering across various screens. Supporting RTL was essential for accessibility and a key milestone in broadening the apps's reach to diverse language communities.
