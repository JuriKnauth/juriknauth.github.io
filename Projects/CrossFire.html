CrossFire

Quick, simple & Massive!

Crossfire is an online tactical first-person shooter that has been around since 2007, but is still going strong today. Due to its popularity in Asia, especially in China and South Korea, it has become one of the most played video games in the world, with a total of 1 billion users in 80 countries worldwide.




Gameplay in Crossfire is intense and fast-paced, with players working together to complete objectives and take down the enemy team. There are a variety of game modes to choose from, each with its own unique objective, and players can customize their loadouts to fit their playstyle. Crossfire is also constantly adding new maps and weapons to keep the experience fresh.
