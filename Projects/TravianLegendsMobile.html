Travian Legends Mobile

A classic strategy MMO reimagined for mobile.

Travian Legends Mobile brings the iconic gameplay of the original browser-based title to iOS and Android. Set in ancient Europe, players lead their tribe to power through diplomacy, trade, and warfare. The mobile version faithfully recreates the core mechanics while modernizing the interface for touchscreens and smaller displays.
