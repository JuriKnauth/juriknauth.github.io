You Are On The Wrong Path

Save the chicks!

The theme of Ludum Dare 46 was Keep it alive. Chicks with brains are thrown onto an assembly line. They will try thier best to save as many chicks as possible! The intensive animal farming industry has no use for male chicks. This factory is desigend to seperate male chicks and dispose them. Any saved chick will grant a point. Try to beat the high score. Single player or two player co-op with points counted for each player. Sadly didnt have enough time to finish the level…
