Hi, I’m Juri: a Unity developer with a passion for building games that feel great to play.

I’ve been creating games for over six years, starting with modding Warcraft 3 and Starcraft 2, and eventually moving into professional studio work and solo development. Today, I work full-time as a Unity Developer on mobile and cross-platform titles, focusing on gameplay, UI/UX, performance, and systems design.

Whether I’m working with a team or on my own projects, I care about the details: the polish, the feedback, and the moment everything just clicks. I love that “A-ha!” moment players get when something works just right, and I enjoy the challenge of making that happen through code, design, or clever systems.

While I’m currently employed full-time, I’m always open to hearing about new opportunities, freelance work, or collaborations that spark my curiosity.
